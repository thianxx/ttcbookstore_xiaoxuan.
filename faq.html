Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bookstore, services, and policies.

What are your opening hours?

We are open daily from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM, including weekends and public holidays.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services within Malaysia only. Free shipping is available for orders above RM50.

Can I return or exchange a book?

Returns and exchanges are accepted within 7 days of purchase with a valid receipt, provided the item is in its original condition.

How can I become a member?

You can sign up for our membership program in-store or online via the Services page. Members enjoy exclusive discounts and early access to sales.
